I am a first-year computer science PhD student at Montana State University. I am
working in the <a href="comptag.github.io" target="_blank"
rel="noopener noreferrer">CompTaG</a> lab under <a href="fasy.us" target="_blank"
rel="noopener noreferrer">Dr. Brittany Terese Fasy</a>. I received my bachelor's
degrees from Montana Technological University in May, 2025. 

**Research Interests**: Broadly, I am interested in computational topology and
algorithms. My current work mainly focuses on balancing theoretical foundations
and practical applications of topological descriptors for data analysis.
